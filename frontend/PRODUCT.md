# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small developer teams managing projects, environments, members, and secrets as part of daily application work. They need to move quickly without losing track of ownership, access, or which configuration belongs to which environment.

## Product Purpose

KeyVault helps teams maintain confident control over secrets and configuration through a simple, reliable workflow. Success means developers can find the right project, switch environments, invite the right collaborators, edit secrets, and verify sensitive values without confusion or unnecessary operational overhead.

## Brand Personality

Calm expert, focused, trustworthy, understated. The product should feel like a precise infrastructure tool that is comfortable to use every day, with confidence coming from clarity rather than noise.

## Anti-references

Avoid enterprise-heavy admin tooling, bureaucratic density, gray-table monotony, and compliance-tool clutter. Do not make the product feel like a generic security dashboard, a marketing site, or a heavy IAM console.

## Design Principles

- Dense but readable: show enough operational context for daily work without turning screens into inventory dumps.
- Confidence over decoration: use hierarchy, states, and copy to make risky actions feel understandable and controlled.
- Clear ownership and action states: make projects, environments, members, pending invites, destructive actions, and unsaved edits visibly distinct.
- Simple workflows first: prefer direct inline actions and focused dialogs over multi-step administration patterns.
- Restrained security-product polish: serious, precise, and trustworthy, with subtle accent color and minimal motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, complete keyboard access, visible focus states, and screen-reader-friendly labels for controls that reveal, edit, copy, or delete secrets. Interactions should be reduced-motion-friendly, avoid relying on color alone for state, and keep sensitive-value workflows clear for users under time pressure.
